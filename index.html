Sculpture and Fabrication

Artist Statement

Balance is rooted in our relationships with people and our environment. My work acts as a tool to generate change in artificial settings and situations that I create, by focusing on the impact of people within them. Balance emerges through a constant, dynamic exchange of interactions. I explore the idea of balance in my immersive kinetic sculptures through symmetrical compositions that, as movement is captured by integrated motion sensors, shift and change as someone approaches. This movement results in a mark in the sand or a change in orientation. My wearable sculptures link people together to highlight their impact on one another. These wearables cannot be worn individually, or else they won't function, communicating how people rely on each other to maintain balance and how change is the process of maintaining this equilibrium.

About Me

Born in the vibrant culture of the Dominican Republic on October 3, 2001, and raised amidst the urban tapestry of Tampa, Florida, I am Gleimi De Jesus—an artist driven by the profound dynamics of human connections. Growing up as the youngest in a family shaped by both Dominican and American influences, I developed a keen interest in the ways cultures intertwine and individuals adapt.

Tampa's urban nature became my canvas, providing insights into how people interact within the intricate web of urban life. What captivates me most is not just what people do, but why they do it. This fascination steered my educational journey into the arts, leading me to explore both painting and sculpture.

While painting captures moments with timeless strokes, sculpture, to me, is a dynamic conversation in three dimensions. It invites viewers to engage physically and emotionally, offering a unique perspective on the interplay of form and space. My work delves into the essence of balance, exploring how individuals impact each other and their surroundings. Through immersive kinetic sculptures and wearable art, I aim to highlight the interconnectedness of humanity and the constant dance of equilibrium.

Join me on this artistic journey as we explore the delicate symphony of relationships, cultural nuances, and the profound reasons behind our actions.
